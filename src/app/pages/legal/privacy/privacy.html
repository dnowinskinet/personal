2. The Short Version

The Site is designed to collect as little visitor information as practical.

  • There are no user accounts, comments, uploads, or website forms.
  • There is no advertising or behavioral tracking.
  • I do not deploy client-side visitor analytics, behavioral analytics, or tracking scripts. Infrastructure providers may provide limited aggregate traffic, performance, and security information derived from ordinary network requests.

  • GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, and related infrastructure providers may process ordinary technical information needed to deliver, protect, and diagnose the Site.

4. Information Stored on Your Device

The Site uses browser local storage to provide requested preferences and game functionality. Depending on how you use the Site, locally stored information may include:

  • Your light or dark appearance preference.
  • Your selected accent color.
  • Sudoku puzzle state, notes, action history, elapsed time, completion status, hint usage, timestamps, and best times.
  • GriftOS progress, unlocks, run state, selected items, timestamps, and audio preferences.
  • Locally generated GriftOS playtest events, game metrics, economy snapshots, and a locally generated session identifier.
  • Older game-save information used to migrate progress from earlier versions.

This information is generally stored only in your browser and is not transmitted to me.

Some game features allow you to export locally stored information as a file or copy a summary to your clipboard. Those actions occur at your direction on your device. The Site does not automatically upload the exported or copied information.

You can remove locally stored information using your browser’s controls for clearing site data or local storage. Clearing site data, using private browsing, changing browsers or devices, or installing certain updates may erase or make saved progress unavailable.
